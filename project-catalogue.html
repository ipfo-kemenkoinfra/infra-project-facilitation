Purpose of the Catalogue

The ICI 2025 Project Catalogue has been prepared to provide a comprehensive overview of infrastructure projects proposed by line ministries, state-owned enterprises, and other institutions within the Government of Indonesia. Developed in conjunction with the International Conference on Infrastructure 2025, this catalogue is intended to foster transparency, facilitate collaboration, and serve as a platform for investment engagement.

Contents of the Catalogue

The catalogue presents:

  • Investors and financial institutionsseeking credible, well-prepared infrastructure pipelines.
  • Private sector developers, operators, and technical consultants interested in partnership opportunities.
  • Policymakers and public officials involved in infrastructure planning, financing, and delivery.
  • Conference delegatesattending thematic sessions, exhibitions, or one-on-one meetings.
